Questions

What exactly am I getting?

One limited-edition print issue of Autostraddle every quarter (four per year), delivered free to your door. Each issue is 200+ pages of essays, photography, reporting, fiction, comics, and queer-themed puzzles.

Can I cancel?

Anytime, in two clicks from your account. No questions, no hidden fees, no auto-renewal traps.

What if I don't love my first issue?

Email us and we'll refund you. Keep the magazine either way.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes — international shipping is available at checkout for an additional fee. Free shipping applies to US orders.

Who's behind it?

Autostraddle is one of the longest-running independent queer media outlets, published by For Them — both led by queer, trans, and lesbian editors, writers, and photographers.
